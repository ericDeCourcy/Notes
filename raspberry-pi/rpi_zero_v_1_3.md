# Raspberry Pi Zero V1.3

### Troublshooting power
When the rpi boots, there should be a green light near the "power" usb port. 

If that power light does not turn on, it may mean boot failure or power failure. [This thread](https://web.archive.org/web/20251105131149/https://forums.raspberrypi.com/viewtopic.php?t=204535) discusses boot failure.

You can test that the board is recieving power by testing the pads on the back side of the power port. [Test procedure blog post](https://www.petervis.com/Raspberry_PI/raspberry-pi-zero/raspberry-pi-zero-will-not-turn-on.html)
