procedure
- drain radiator
	- tern the petcock
	- drain it
	- unturn the petcock
- remove radiator hose
- swap out thermostat
- take a bunch of pics of where the belt is routed
	- use flash
	- check pics are good before continueing
- untension tensioner
- remove belt
- remove water pump
- clean mating surface
- put gasket into pump
	- apply some grease to keep gasket in place
- put pump on
- with belt off, the pulleys and make sure none have any play in them
- torque to spec
	- whats the spec?
- put belt on
- tension tensioner
- refill coolant
	- refill from radiator, then run truck to let the thermostat open and pull water in
	- then top off radiator
	- then top off reservoir 
- burp the air 
	- run truck, light idle for a few mins then light neighborhood driving until up to temp
  - check that temp has been hit by feeling upper radiator hose. Hot hose means thermostat has opened
  - let is sit overnight, refill radiator and put cap on. Refill reservoir to between min/max lines

- how to re-route belt?
	- after done, check that belt isn't riding lips of belts
	- make sure that every ribbed pulley had belt fulley seated


While youre there...	
- removing the airbox
	- check the air filter and replace if needed
- wiggle pulleys and look for any play in rotation. Rotation should be clean and true, only should move on rotational axis


Torque specs
- water pump mounting bolts - 18 ft-lbs
- tensioner mounting bolt - 30 ft-lbs
